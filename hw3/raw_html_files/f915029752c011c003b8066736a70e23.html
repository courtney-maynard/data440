Michele the Trainer’s Sexy Organic Potato Soup

This soup could have been sexier garnished with a sprig of dill, but not having any dill was my dilemma (get it?).

You can achieve this with any combo of hot and not hot pepper, but find a not hot pepper known for flavor.

Gather-Boil-Blend-Serve…that’s how we roll

Do you have an immersion blender?  That is the best choice for this recipe, or you can ladel into big blender and then transfer back, but that’s a bigger hassle.  If you have a Vita-mix, easier (I actually used a very lame blender and transferred, so any blender you have is better than the one I used, and my soup still rocked!)

Gather:
Organic Potatoes (5 large)
Organic Celery Root (2 small)
1 dried Organic Chipotle Pepper (hotter)
1 fresh Organic Poblano Pepper (more flavor than heat)
1/2 – 1 Organic Celery (with leaves, chopped)
1/2 Organic Purple Onion
Organic Carrots (3-5)
Organic Garlic (3 cloves)
Organic Bay Leaves (3)

Add To Taste:

Celtic Sea Salt,
Organic Ground Black Pepper and
A. Vogel Brand Organic Herbamare, the green one (Season Salt)  <<This is not critical, it’s just what I had on hand.  Using only Celtic Sea Salt will be find.  Please avoid any seasonings with MSG.>>

  • Boil until potatoes are done
  • Remove Chipotle and save, discard bay leaves
  • Blend the rest with immersion blender
  • Now you can replace that Chipotle, if you want (it’s identifiable if someone wants to eat or remove it)
  • Optional;  Add 1-2 Tablespoons of Organic Raw Almond Butter to each bowl after serving, if desired (stir it until it’s creamy than swirl it in)
  • Optional:  Got Dill?  Garnish with a piece of Dill and a shake of Paprika (of course both Organic)

Yes, you can eat some potatoes and still maintain your weight!  I don’t share recipes because I’m a domestic who wanted to write a cookbook.  I share recipes because I know there is a better way to lose and maintain weight loss, and build muscle!
