    Police: Ohio man's death now being investigated as a homicide in Springfield

    By Tom Ackerman, Springfield State Journal- Register,

    14 days ago

    (This story was updated to add new information.)

    The death of a 29-year-old man in Springfield is now being called a homicide.

    Springfield police said the man, identified as Telley Palmer of Columbus, Ohio, was found with multiple gunshot wounds in the 400 block of South Illinois Street around 2:30 a.m. Palmer was taken to the hospital where he died from his injuries.

    In a news release, police said the shooting happened after a fight involving multiple people at a Springfield bar earlier in the night. Police said they were not notified of the fight.

    Police said at least three different guns were used and officers found 35 shell casings in the area.

    Police have not said if they have any suspects as of Monday afternoon.

    The shooting is under investigation.
