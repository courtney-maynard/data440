Live Webinar: Responsible AI Adoption by Philanthropic Organizations

Event Details

  • Date/Time: Wednesday, October 16th, 2024 from 1:00 pm to 2:00 pm
  • Categories: Firm-Hosted Events

Join us for an insightful webinar exploring the growing use of artificial intelligence (AI) within private foundations and the philanthropic community. Jean Westrick, Executive Director of the Technology Association of Grantmakers, will share how grantmakers can effectively use AI and establish policies to create a stronger impact for those they serve.

Key Topics:

  • Introduction to AI
  • How AI is used in philanthropy
  • Framework for responsible adoption
  • How AI improves impact and efficiencies of organizations

Who Should Attend:
Foundation Board Members, Executives, Chief Financial Officers, Chief Technology Officers, Controllers, Program Officers and other Private Foundation Professionals.

Featured Speaker:
Technology Association of Grantmakers
Jean Westrick, Executive Director 

Hosts:
PKF O’Connor Davies
Anan Samara, EA, Partner
Scott Brown, CPA, Partner

CPE Credit:
1 CPE credit in Specialized Knowledge
No prerequisites or advanced planning required
Program Level: Basic
Delivery Method: Group internet based

For information regarding complaint and/or program cancellation policies, please contact l&dsupport@pkfod.com.

PKF O’Connor Davies is registered with the National Association of State Boards of Accountancy (NASBA) as a sponsor of continuing professional education on the National Registry of CPE Sponsors. State Boards of Accountancy have the final authority on the acceptance of individual courses for CPE Credit. Complaints regarding registered sponsors may be submitted to the National Registry of CPE Sponsors through its website: www.nasbaregistry.org.
