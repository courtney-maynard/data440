Lightbits at OpenInfra Days

Use the form at the right to request a demonstration of Lightbits high-performance, software-defined storage for clouds built on OpenStack and OpenShift platforms at OpenInfra Days and learn how to build a high-performance, cost-efficient, scalable cloud service.

Block storage is a fundamental building block of cloud services. Modern applications and high-performance databases demand more performance at a greater scale than ever before. Storage can either be an accelerator or a bottleneck for real-time analytics and transactional workloads.

Unlike cloud services built on direct-attached storage (DAS) or storage-attached networks (SAN), Lightbits provides:

  • Rapid deployment and expansion
  • The highest performance and lowest consistent latency at scale
  • High availability and resiliency
  • Effective price/performance

See us at OpenInfra Days to learn more.
