Mea Pregnancy Support Services is a faith-based organization and provides support to anyone facing an unexpected pregnancy or pregnancy related difficulty. We are committed to unconditional acceptance of everyone we serve, regardless of their choices.
All services are completely free and confidential. Our support is peer-based and not intended to substitute for professional counselling.
We are not a medical clinic. We do not provide or assist in arranging for abortions. We are not an adoption agency. We do not facilitate adoptions.
