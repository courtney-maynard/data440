How to use these resources
A comprehensive collection of materials and tools to help educate and inform the public about bird flu, including fact sheets, posters, and infographics. These resources aim to promote awareness and understanding of bird flu prevention, protection, and transmission.
Print Resources are available in multiple languages, including Spanish, Haitian-Creole, Nahuatl, K'iche', and Chinese. Enter the language in the "search" field to obtain a list of associated print resources in these languages.
